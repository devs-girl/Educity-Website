US universities with the highest acceptance rates

Top-tier universities and the ivy league schools in the USA have the lowest acceptance rate due to high demand. The entry requirements are always high

Here’s a list of top schools in the USA and their acceptance rate (from high to low). The list is for the fall intake of the year.

How to apply to these top universities?

The step-by-step process for universities with the highest acceptance rates is pretty standard. You need to make sure you

  • Visit the official website of the university to which you want to apply.
  • Create a candidate profile using your mobile number or email credentials.
  • Complete your profile by uploading the necessary documents.
  • Explore the intake process and the courses offered in each intake. You can look out for the USA, UK, and Canada intake process here.
  • Shortlist the course you want to pursue and pay the application fee. The application fee varies depending on the university and the course you choose.
  • Finally, apply and track via your account. You might have to attend a virtual interview some universities conduct.
